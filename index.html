Section 02 — Onboarding

What to Expect

Your Marathon engagement is modular. Toggle below to mark which services are active for your account.

📊
Active
Marathon Software

Your dedicated performance intelligence platform — real-time dashboards, incrementality-adjusted ROAS, and reporting built for how your team actually makes decisions.

What it is
A proprietary SaaS dashboard giving you live access to campaign performance, channel-level spend efficiency, and incrementality-adjusted metrics — always on, always current.
What we need from you
Ad platform credentials (read access), pixel or conversion event data, and a technical onboarding contact. Data access form shared Day 1.
First 2 weeks
Account provisioned → data connections live → baseline audit delivered. Login access + guided walkthrough by end of Week 1.
🎯
Active
Full Funnel Services

End-to-end media strategy and execution across paid search, paid social, programmatic, and emerging channels — tuned for incrementality, not attribution credit.

What it is
Marathon acts as your performance media arm — building and executing channel strategy, managing campaign activation, and optimizing spend allocation against true ROAS signals.
What we need from you
Monthly budget ranges, channel exclusions or existing vendor relationships, brand guidelines, and campaign history. Media brief template shared in Week 1.
First 2 weeks
Channel audit complete → media plan drafted and reviewed → first campaigns structured for launch by Day 14. No spend goes live without your sign-off.
✦
Active
Creative Generation

Performance-native ad creative — concepted, produced, and iterated by Marathon's creative team in lockstep with your media strategy. Built to test, not to hang in a gallery.

What it is
Full creative production including static, video, and motion across all active channels. Each asset ships with a testing hypothesis — creative decisions driven by data.
What we need from you
Brand guidelines, existing asset library, tone-of-voice doc, and an approval stakeholder. Iteration cycles run on a 5-business-day turnaround once workflow is set up.
First 2 weeks
Creative brief reviewed → first batch of 6–8 launch assets in review by Day 10. Approval workflow shared via [TOOL] on Day 1 of kickoff.
📍
Active
Geo Holdouts

Scientifically rigorous incrementality measurement — pairing test markets against matched holdout regions to isolate the true causal lift of your media investment.

What it is
A geo-level matched-market test where selected DMAs receive media while comparable markets are held dark. The delta reveals your true incremental ROAS — no model dependency.
What we need from you
Historical sales or conversion data by geography (DMA, ZIP, or state), regional constraints, and sign-off on test duration (typically 4–8 weeks minimum).
First 2 weeks
Test market analysis run → holdout pairs selected and reviewed → test design document shared for sign-off. Execution begins with first media live date.
